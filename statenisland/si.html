Places to go to

Snug Harbor Cultural Center & Botanical Garden

A historic campus with gardens, museums, and cultural institutions

Staten Island Ferry

Free ferry service between Staten Island and Manhattan, offering views of the Statue of Liberty and the city skyline

Historic Richmond Town

A living history village and museum complex portraying Staten Island's history from the colonial period to the present

Staten Island Greenbelt

A network of natural areas and parks covering nearly 3,000 acres, providing hiking trails, wildlife habitats, and recreational opportunities
